Who am I?

Alireza Tehrani

Bachelor Student at Polytechnic University of Tehran

I was born in Tehran, Iran. I studied Mathematics and Physics at Mobtakeran High school, one of the well-known schools in the whole region. Later on, I got my first bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering and decided to continue as a science high school teacher in my passionate subject, Mathematics. I have been working in my beloved career, guiding students to reach their goals and expand their skills and knowledge for roughly ten years. During this memorable journey, the more I used programming to plan lessons and create fun learning tools for students, the more I became interested in Computer Science and Programming. It was not until after I taught a few Computer online courses that I realized Computer Science is what I would love to study and work in for the rest of my life. As motivated as I was, I took part in the university entrance exam for the new major. Hopefully, I was admitted to Tehran Polytechnic University for the Computer Engineering program. I started my second bachelor's degree with far more passion and enthusiasm than before.


Soon, I hold a great passion for Computer Science, particularly AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science. Although I have been awarded a Master's position at Amirkabir University, I would like to continue my studies in a high-ranking university to flourish my knowledge and have more practical experience. Therefore, I'm looking forward to continuing my education at the Ph.D. level and joining a professor's lab with a shared research interest.


I know that I'm yet at the beginning of a long journey, but standing along the way with a lot of courage, strength, spirit, and enthusiasm to move on and unfold new chapters in my life.
