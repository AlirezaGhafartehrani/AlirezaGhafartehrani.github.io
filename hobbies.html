Hobbies

Since childhood, I have always had a great passion for sports. I started professional wrestling by the age of fifteen and continued to participate in different competitions. After growing up, I fulfilled my enthusiasm by mixing these skills with martial arts and doing Wushu. My last minor achievement was winning the championship in Provincial Wushu Competitions in 2019 among all college students. However, I deeply believe achievements are not just about ranks but also about moral attitudes and manners. My most notable outcome was the honor to win the Ethics Award for Fair Wrestling in the Provincial Competition during this whole time.


Honestly, I have always advocated sports since physical activities relieve tension and prepare the mind for more mental activities. I always try to manage my time to schedule for: Running, jogging, Mountain climbing, swimming, playing Volleyball, or maybe lifting weights.
